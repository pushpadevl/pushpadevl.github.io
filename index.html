About Me

I am Pushpendra, a cryptogrpaphy student, a part-time photographer and the creator of this website. I do various other things to keep my life interesting but there is something omnipresent among all of them; it's the bug of curiosity and the will to always learn something new, not just academically but in every way my body and brain allows.

To affix my academic standing, I have a Bachelors in Electronics and Communications Engineering, Master of Science in Economics and just recently received a Master of Technology in Cryptology and Security. The first two degrees were offered by Birla Institute of Technology and Science Pilani, while the last one was offered by Indian Statistical Institute, Kolkata. At ISI, I experienced something I had never experienced before, the autonomy along with no financial worries and best of all, people who share a similar thought process as mine.
